Send money via email and messaging, without any fee!!

HashPay is an experimental e-cash that enables decentralized, instant payments to anyone, anywhere in the whole-wide world. Users send HashPay "cash" to one another directly by copying-and-pasting their HashPay "Cash" to their recipient, No server or network is required, meaning payments can be made offline

Receive/Send

Receive Cash Send Cash

Receiving HashPay: Open the HashPay site, and click Receive, then paste the HashPay Cash.

Making payments using HashPay: Copy and paste the generated HashPay cash from the site and send it to someone else over secure email, txty, whsprr chat, or any other method.
